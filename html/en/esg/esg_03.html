Code of Ethics
With customer satisfaction as our top priority,
we receive customer trust by improving the quality of products
and services for transparent and trusted corporate management.
We handle business based on good faith,
comply with relevant laws and regulations, and comply with the will and order of commerce.
We carry out our work with honesty and sincere passion,
and lead work innovation based on professional knowledge.
We defend the honor and personal dignity
of the company and do not demand or receive any form
of financial or non-monetary benefit from our customers and counterparties.
We do not use the company's property for personal gain.
If personal interests conflict while performing duties, the company's interests are first considered, judged, and used.
