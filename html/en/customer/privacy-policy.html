Privacy Policy
Korea Precursor Co. handles your personal information in a secure way.
Korea Precursor Co.(referred to as the "Company") complies with the personal information protection regulations under relevant laws and regulations, such as the Personal Information Protection Act and the Act on Promotion of Information and Communications Network Utilization and Information Protection, and does its best to protect the rights and interests of information subjects such as customers, employees, and website users by establishing a privacy policy and handling policy in accordance with relevant laws and regulations. The Company informs the information subject of the purpose and manner in which the personal information provided by the information subject is processed through the privacy policy, and what measures are being taken to protect the personal information. The Company will notify the information subject of any revisions to the privacy policy through the notice on the website or notify individually
01. General
"Personal information" refers to information about a living individual, including information that allows the individual to be recognized through name and image (including information that can be easily combined with other information to recognize a specific individual, even if the information alone does not allow the individual to be recognized). "Information Subject" means a person who can be recognized by the information processed and who is the subject of the information. The Company takes measures to ensure that the Privacy Policy is always readily available by disclosing it on the first screen of the homepage (http://www.kpc.co.kr) , and if the Privacy Policy is revised, it will be notified through the website announcement or notify individually.
02. Personal Information Processing Items and Purpose
The Company does not process sensitive information that may significantly infringe on the privacy of the information subject or uniquely identifiable information granted to uniquely distinguish individuals without the provisions of laws and regulations or the consent of the information subject.
  • A. Processing Items
    • Email, contact information, country, company name, department, name
  • B. Purpose of processing
    • Contact information to investigate facts and deliver answers to customer inquiries
03. Processing and retention period of personal information
After the purpose of collecting and using personal information is fulfilled, the information shall be destroyed without delay.
04. Method of destruction of personal information
Personal information stored in the form of electronic files shall be destroyed by using a technical method that cannot reproduce the records. Personal information printed on paper shall be destroyed by shredding or incineration.
05. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties
The Company uses personal information within the scope of the purpose of collection, and does not use personal information beyond the scope or provide or disclose it to third parties, except in the following cases.
  • When separate consent is obtained from the information subject
  • If there are special provisions in other law
  • When the information subject or his/her legal representative is in a state where he/she is unable to express his/her will or cannot obtain prior consent due to unknown address, and it is clearly recognized that it is necessary for the urgent interests of the life, body, or property of the information subject or a third party.
  • When personal information is provided in a form that does not recognize a specific individual as necessary for purposes such as statistical compilation and academic research.
06. Consignment of Personal Information Processing
The Company will not share or entrust personal information to any external entity unless the information subject has provided their consent for the same. However, if such an entrustment occurs, the Company will ensure that all necessary measures are taken in accordance with relevant laws and regulations, to guarantee the safe and secure management of personal information.
  • Consignee: Sorin Information Technology Co.
  • Contents of consignment: Recruitment and website system consignment operation
07. Rights and obligations of the information subject and how to exercise them
All information subjects may request access, correction, deletion, and suspension of their personal information processed by the Company. However, the Company may refuse or limit the above requests in the following cases.
  • There are special provisions in the law or it is unavoidable to comply with legal obligations.
  • If there is a risk of harming the life or body of another person or unreasonably infringing on the property and other interests of another person.
  • When it is difficult to fulfill the contract, such as not being able to provide the services contracted with the information subject, and the information subject has not clearly expressed his/her intention to terminate the contract.
Methods and procedures for exercising rights
An information subject who wishes to access personal information may submit a request for access, correction, deletion, or suspension of processing in writing or by e-mail. (See "09. Civil Service for Personal Information" for the department in charge.) The Company will take the corresponding measures within 10 days unless there is a justifiable reason, and if there is a reason for refusal or restriction, the Company will notify the reason for restriction or refusal and the method of appeal within 5 days. When the information subject or his/her representative requests access to personal information, the Company may verify the identity of the information subject or his/her representative by checking identification documents such as resident registration cards and authorized electronic signatures.
08. Technical, administrative, and physical protection measures for personal information
In handling personal information, the Company takes the following safety measures to ensure that personal information is not lost, stolen, leaked, altered or damaged.
  • Technical measures
    • The Company complies with the standards set by laws and regulations for the safe storage and transmission of personal information.
    • The Company takes measures to prevent damage caused by computer viruses by using antivirus programs. The antivirus program is updated periodically, and if a virus suddenly appears, we provide a vaccine as soon as it is available to prevent personal information from being compromised.
    • In preparation for external intrusions such as hacking, we use an intrusion prevention system and a vulnerability analysis system for each server to ensure security.
  • Administrative Measures
    • The Company restricts access to personal information to those who perform sales and marketing tasks directly with the information subject, those who perform personal information management tasks, and those who are required to handle personal information for other business purposes.
    • The Company conducts regular in-house and outsourced training on personal information protection for employees who handle personal information, and thoroughly manages and supervises them to comply with laws and regulations on personal information protection.
  • Physical Measures
    • To ensure the safe storage of personal information and personal information processing systems, we take protective measures to prevent physical access, such as locks.
    • Computer rooms and data storage rooms are set as special protection areas and access is controlled..
