Rejection of unauthorized collection of email addresses
Korea Precursor Co. refuses to collect unauthorized e-mail addresses posted on this website using e-mail collection programs or other technical devices in order to protect customer information and block indiscriminate external mailing, and please note that violation of this will be criminally punished under the Information and Communications Network Act.
Article 1 (Purpose of processing personal information, personal information processing and retention period, items of personal information processed, etc.)
Article 50.2 (Prohibition of unauthorized collection of e-mail addresses, etc.)
  • ① No person shall collect e-mail addresses using a program or other technical device that automatically collects e-mail addresses from the Internet homepage without the prior consent of the operator or administrator of the Internet homepage.
  • ② No person shall sell or distribute e-mail addresses collected in violation of Paragraph (1).
  • ③ No person shall use an e-mail address for information transmission knowing that it is an e-mail address prohibited from collection, sale, and distribution under Paragraphs 1 and 2.
Article 74 (Penalties)
  • ① Any person who falls under any of the following items shall be punished by imprisonment for not more than one year or a fine of not more than 10 million won.
    • 5. a person who collects, sells, distributes, or uses an e-mail address to transmit information in violation of Article 50-2.
