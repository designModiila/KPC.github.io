Precursor

A key material for the

secondary battery industry

representing eco-friendly energy

A precursor is a chemical compound, used as a raw material in the manufacturing process of anode materials for batteries. The composition of the anode material determines the characteristics and uses of the battery, and precursors are key raw materials that account for more than 70% of the cost of the anode material.
Securing sustainability by supplying eco-friendly precursors and linking them to recycling
Korea Precursor Co. produces eco-friendly secondary battery materials that secure sustainability by renewable raw materials through recycling processes and linkages. Starting mass production in 2024, the company plans to manufacture1,500 tons of lithium hydroxide by utilizing 20,000 tons/year of precursor production and 6,000 tons/year of recycled raw materials.
Reducing dependence on precursor imports, realizing localization of precursors
In the domestic precursor market, which has been dependent on imports for more than 90%, we will secure profitability and localize precursors by designing a differentiated recycling integration process based on decades of metal extraction technology know-how.

Differentiated technological competitiveness, advanced research and accumulated know-how

The formulation of precursors requires a long period of research and know-how that determines the lifespan and cost performance of the finished battery.
We supply competitive and eco-friendly precursors with increased productivity by applying the latest precursor mass production process with technology to control the proportion of raw materials to increase battery capacity and reduce costs.

Price competitiveness by utilizing industrial infrastructure and stability by establishing our own raw material supply chain
The secondary battery material industry has been recognized for its growth potential with a high growth rate as the demand for these materials is rapidly expanding, mainly due to the growth of the electric vehicle market in response to the global trend of climate change.

Korea Precursor Co. has established its own raw material supply chain to respond to the growth of the market, and is not affected by external factors that affect the supply and demand of raw materials, giving it an edge in supply stability and price competitiveness.
