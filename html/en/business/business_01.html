Precursor

It is a key material of the

secondary battery industry

representing eco-friendly energy

A precursor is a compound that becomes a raw material in the process of manufacturing a cathode material for batteries. The characteristics and use of a battery depend on what compound the cathode material is composed of. Among them, the precursor is a key raw material that accounts for more than 70% of the cathode material cost.
Securing sustainability by supplying eco-friendly precursors and linking recycling
Korea precursor Co., Ltd. produces eco-friendly secondary battery materials that secure sustainability by utilizing renewable raw materials through linkage with the recycling process. Starting with commissioning in 2023 and mass production in 2024, we will secure a production capacity of 1,500 tons of precursor raw materials and lithium hydroxide using 20,000 tons per year of precursor and 6,000 tons per year of recycling.

Differentiated technological competitiveness, advanced research and accumulated know-how

The combination of cathode material precursors requires long research and know-how to determine the life and cost performance of the finished battery.
In addition to the technology to control the proportion of raw materials to increase battery capacity and reduce costs, we apply the latest precursor mass production process to supply competitive eco-friendly precursors with increased productivity.

Securing price competitiveness using industrial infrastructure and stability through the establishment of its own raw material supply chain
The secondary battery material industry is recognized for its growth potential by recording a high growth rate as demand rapidly expands around the growth of the electric vehicle market in line with the global trend of responding to climate change.

Korea Global Co., Ltd. is establishing its own raw material supply and demand network to proactively respond to the growth of the market, and is not affected by external factors related to the supply and demand of raw materials, so it can gain an advantage in supply stability and price competitiveness.
