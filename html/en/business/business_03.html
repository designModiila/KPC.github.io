Recycle & Precursor production process
Waste Badger Scrap
Waste batteries and cathode scraps are collected and pulverized to manufacture them in the form of black sand and black powder.
Recall NCM
Nickel, cobalt, and manganese (NCM) contained in the raw materials made through Scrap are recovered through a series of processes.
Secondary raw material input
The recovered NCM (secondary raw material) is stored in the raw material tank in the form of an aqueous solution and put into the precursor production process.
Reaction/Filtration process
By inducing precursor synthesis through coprecipitation reaction, the synthesized precursor and reaction filtrate are separated and the precursor is extracted.
Drying/Mixing process
After drying and removing moisture in the filtered precursor, the extracted precursor is uniformly mixed.
Classification/De-ironing process
After selecting a precursor that meets the standard, remove magnetic foreign substances through an electronic de-ironer to complete a high purity precursor.

Blending Process
Nickel (Ni), cobalt (Co), and manganese (Mn) are dissolved to form a metal solution, and then mixed according to the standards established through long research to prepare an aqueous NCM solution.
Reaction Process
An aqueous NCM solution and reagents (NH4OH, NaOH) are added to the reactor to synthesize the precursor through a coprecipitation reaction.
Filtration Process
The synthesized precursor and the reaction filtrate (餘厄) are separated to extract the precursor.
Drying/Mixing
Process
Dry and remove moisture from the filtered precursor above a certain level, and then mix the precursors extracted from the reactor uniformly.
Classification/
De-ironing Process
If you select precursors that meet strict particle size standards and remove magnetic foreign substances that can be introduced during the process through an electronic de-ironer, a high-purity precursor containing technology unique to Korea Precursor Co., Ltd. will be completed.
A quick look at the production process
I'll briefly explain
the precursor production process.
Firing Process
BS (black sand) and BP (black powder), which are manufactured by collecting and grinding end-of-life batteries and cathode materials Scraps, are put into a firing furnace and processed in an inert atmosphere.
Melting/
Filtration Process
Dissolve nickel (Ni), cobalt (Co), manganese (Mn), and lithium (Li) contained in the fired raw material to prepare a metal solution, and filter it to separate the dissolved solution and non-dissolved residue.
NCM Recovery
Process
Nickel, cobalt, and manganese contained in the solution are precipitated using reagents (NaOH) to separate NCM, and then the NCM solution is produced through secondary dissolution and purification processes.
Li Recovery
Process
The NCM-removed solution undergoes evaporation concentration and purification to produce lithium hydroxide.
Supply of CM
aqueous solution
and lithium hydroxide
The produced NCM solution is moved to the raw material tank and used as a precursor production raw material, and lithium hydroxide is packaged and supplied to the consumer.
